STANDARD PROCUREMENT POLICY v1.2

1. Competitive Procurement
Purchases above PHP 100,000 require at least three supplier quotations.

2. Purchase Authorization
Every procurement transaction must include:
- Purchase Request (PR)
- Purchase Order (PO)
- Invoice
- Delivery Receipt
- Authorized Approver

3. IT Equipment
IT purchases exceeding PHP 200,000 require written approval from the IT Director.
Only approved vendors may be used.

4. Medical Supplies
Purchases exceeding:
- 30 stethoscopes
- 50 digital thermometers

require written inventory justification.

5. Vendor Compliance
All vendors must have:
- Business Registration
- Tax Identification
- Accreditation

6. Financial Controls
Future-dated transactions are prohibited unless specifically authorized.

7. Audit Thresholds
The following automatically require manual review:
- Missing supporting documents
- Missing approvals
- Large purchases
- Policy threshold exceptions
- Vendor accreditation issues
